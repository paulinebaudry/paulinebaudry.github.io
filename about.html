Hi there!


My name is Pauline and I am a Franco-Swedish data enthusiast living in Paris.

After four years in London, completing an undergraduate degree in Arts and Sciences (BASc) and a Master's in Smart Cities and Urban Analytics from the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA) at University College London, I moved back to Paris in 2019.

I joined the DataCity team at NUMA, working for a year on innovative solutions to urban challenges with different partners - corporate groups, startups and experts from the City of Paris. I am currently working at RATP, the transport operator in Paris, on innovative and digital projects.

I am passionate about data storytelling, using different methods to produce graphics and various forms of visualisations.

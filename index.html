Hey there!

My name is Pauline and I am a Franco-Swedish city and data enthusiast living in Paris.

After four years in London, completing an undegraduate degree in Arts and Sciences (BASc) and a Masters in Smart Cities and Urban Analytics from the Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA) at University College London, I moved back to Paris where I grew up. I recently worked at NUMA on the DataCity programme, working on innovative solutions to urban challenges with different partners - corporate groups, startups and experts from the City of Paris.

I am passionate about all initiatives making cities more efficient, resilient and enjoyable.
